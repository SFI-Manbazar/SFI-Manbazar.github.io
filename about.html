Who We Are ?

Welcome to SFI Manbazar LC

SFI Manbazar LC is dedicated to empowering students and advocating for social justice, educational reforms, and equal rights. Through initiatives like Red Volunteers, they provide humanitarian aid, health services, and relief efforts during crises. SFI actively fights for affordable education, unites students and educators, and works alongside CPI(M) to promote equality, progress, and societal upliftment.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission & Vision

We strive to empower students, advocate for equal education, and fight for social justice. Our vision is to create a world where education is a fundamental right, and every student can contribute to building a better future.

Champion Students' Rights

We focus on protecting students' rights, ensuring their voices are heard. Through collective efforts, we aim to bring transformative changes in education.
