Discussion On Student Rights

The SFI Manbazar Local Committee is organizing a Discussion On Student Rights on 27th October 2024. This event will provide a platform for students, educators, and community leaders to come together to address the critical issues surrounding student rights, access to education, and the challenges faced by students today.

The discussion will cover a wide range of topics including the need for free and equitable access to education, the role of student activism, and strategies for safeguarding the rights of students in today's socio-political environment. It will serve as an opportunity to reinforce our commitment to Independence, Democracy, and Socialism and to empower students to stand up for their rights.

  • Exploring the challenges and opportunities in student activism.
  • Strategies for ensuring educational access and affordability for all.
  • Protecting the rights of students and advocating for systemic change.
  • An open forum for sharing ideas and planning future initiatives.

We invite all students, educators, and members of the community to join this event to ensure that the voices of students are heard, and to work collectively toward a brighter and more equitable future.
