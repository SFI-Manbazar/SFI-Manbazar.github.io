  • FAQ
  • How can I participate in voting campaigns organized by SFI?

    SFI Manbazar LC organizes awareness campaigns around voting rights. To participate, you can reach out to your local SFI committee members or follow our announcements on upcoming events. Volunteering is open to everyone, and training sessions are available to guide you through the process.

  • How can I choose a political party to support?

    SFI believes in educating individuals about the importance of understanding political ideologies before supporting a party. We encourage youth to read, participate in discussions, and be part of educational seminars that help make informed choices. Visit our events page for the latest updates.

  • How can I become a volunteer for SFI Manbazar LC?

    Becoming a volunteer for SFI Manbazar LC is simple. Fill out the registration form available on our website, or contact us directly. Volunteers help in organizing events, spreading awareness, and assisting in various social activities. Training is provided for all volunteers.

  • From where should I start if I want to donate to SFI causes?

    Donating to SFI Manbazar LC can be done through various channels. You can start by visiting our official donation page, where you will find details about the causes we are currently supporting. Your contribution can help fund education initiatives, health drives, and other social programs.

  • How much should I donate to support SFI's initiatives?

    There is no set amount for donations. SFI Manbazar LC welcomes contributions of any size, whether small or large. Every donation helps us support the welfare of students and the broader community. Feel free to contact us if you have specific preferences for how your donation should be used.
