Instructions for Payment

  • Open your preferred payment app (Google Pay or PhonePe).
  • Scan the respective QR code to initiate the payment.
  • Enter the amount you wish to donate and complete the payment.
  • After completing the transaction, note down the UTR (Unique Transaction Reference) or Transaction ID.
  • Fill out the form below with your details and the UTR to confirm your donation.

Please Fill Up The Form

We need your details to process your donation and provide you with a receipt. Please ensure all fields are filled accurately.

Important Information:

  • After your donation, we will verify the details you've provided in the form.
  • Upon successful verification, a receipt will be sent to you via WhatsApp and email for your records.
  • If the verification is unsuccessful or additional information is required, we will contact you through your provided phone number, email, or WhatsApp to clarify the donation details.
  • We ensure full transparency, and you will receive a confirmation once your donation is successfully processed.
  • If you have any queries or concerns regarding your donation, feel free to reach out to us.
