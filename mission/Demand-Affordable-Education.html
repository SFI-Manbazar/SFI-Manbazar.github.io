Demand Affordable Education: Bridging the Gap for Equal Opportunity

At the SFI Manbazar Local Committee, we are committed to the cause of affordable education for all. The rising costs of tuition and educational materials have become significant barriers for many students, leaving them unable to pursue their dreams of academic success.

We believe that education is a fundamental right and should be accessible to everyone regardless of their socio-economic background. By fighting for policies that reduce the cost of education and increase public funding for schools and universities, we aim to create a more inclusive and equitable society.

Our mission is to challenge the unjust systems that prioritize profit over education. We are actively engaged in campaigns that call for reductions in tuition fees, free learning resources, and financial aid programs that support underprivileged students.

Affordable education is not just a goal; it's a necessity for social progress. We urge everyone to join us in this fight, from students to educators and policymakers. Together, we can demand an education system that values learning over wealth and opportunity over privilege.

  • Advocating for reduced tuition fees and eliminating hidden charges
  • Ensuring free or affordable access to textbooks and learning materials
  • Pushing for government-sponsored scholarships and financial aid programs
  • Fighting for increased investment in public education institutions
  • Promoting debt-free education to relieve students from the burden of loans

The future of education must be affordable and accessible to all. Our continuous advocacy, combined with community support, aims to make this vision a reality.
