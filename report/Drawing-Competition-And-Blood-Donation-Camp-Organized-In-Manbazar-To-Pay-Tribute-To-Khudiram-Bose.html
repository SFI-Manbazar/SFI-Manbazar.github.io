Drawing Competition and Blood Donation Camp Organized in Manbazar to Pay Tribute to Khudiram Bose

On 11th August 2024, the SFI & DYFI Manbazar Local Committee organized a Drawing Competition and Blood Donation Camp in Manbazar to honor the legacy of Khudiram Bose on his death anniversary. The event, filled with enthusiasm and participation from people of all ages, was a tribute to the young revolutionary who laid down his life for India's freedom struggle.

The day started with a drawing competition for children, which saw a large number of participants. The children, equipped with colors and drawing boards, displayed their creativity by sketching various themes related to patriotism and freedom. The atmosphere was filled with energy and excitement as young minds channeled their artistic talents, while parents and community members cheered them on.

Homage to Khudiram Bose, youngest revolutionary and one of the youngest martyrs of India's freedom struggle who laid his life for the motherland at the age of 18.

By: SFI & DYFI

At the same venue, a blood donation camp was also organized, encouraging people to come forward and donate blood for a noble cause. Many donors participated, offering their support to save lives and help the needy. The blood donation drive received positive responses from the community, with local volunteers ensuring everything was well-coordinated.

The event was not only a tribute to Khudiram Bose but also an effort to instill values of patriotism, responsibility, and community service in the younger generation. The organizing committee thanked all participants and volunteers for making the event a success and reminded everyone of the importance of giving back to society.

The day ended with a heartfelt tribute to Khudiram Bose, remembering his bravery and sacrifice for the nation. The SFI & DYFI Manbazar Local Committee hopes to continue such events in the future to keep the spirit of patriotism alive among the people.

The event concluded with a message of unity and the importance of youth participation in nation-building. The organizers emphasized that the spirit of Khudiram Bose lives on through such initiatives, inspiring future generations to contribute to society. They vowed to keep organizing similar events, fostering a sense of community and responsibility.
